Lasagna

Description:

This Lasagna is a delicious but simple recipe for anyone to follow.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Lasagna Sauce
  • Cooking Oil
  • Large Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • 500g Pork Mince
  • Two Tins of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Pint of Milk
  • One tbsp of Cornflour
  • One tbsp of Butter

Steps

  1. Chop Onion and Bell Pepper.
  2. Add the Cornflour and Butter to a pan and mix under a gentle heat to make a roux.
  3. Once the roux has formed add the milk and most of the cheese. Leave under low heat mixing often for the remainder of cooking.
  4. Wet a new pan with the cooking oil. Brown Mince, Onions and Bell Peppers.
  5. Add the Lasagna Sauce and Tins of Chopped Tomatoes and mix in.
  6. Leave to simmer for 15 minutes. Start pre-heating oven to 180°C.
  7. Place one third of the mixture into a large tray and spread evenly. Top with a layer of lasagna sheets.
  8. Add thin layer of the cheese sauce. Top with a layer of lasagna sheets.
  9. Keep alternating until all the ingredients are used up, be sure to end with a layer of cheese sauce with no sheets atop.
  10. Grate and sprinkle the remainder of the remainder of the cheese on the top.
  11. Cook in the oven for 20 minutes.
  12. Remove and enjoy!
