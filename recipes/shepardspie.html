Shepard's Pie

Description:

This Shepard's Pie is a delicious but simple recipe for anyone to follow.

Ingredients

  • Some cooking oil of your choice
  • Large Onion
  • 500g Pork Mince
  • Handful of peas
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • Coleman's Shepard's Pie Mix
  • Beef Oxo Cube
  • 490ml Hot Water
  • Cold water
  • 800g Potatoes
  • 500g Mature Cheddar Cheese
  • Optional: Bell Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and place in a pan of water, and leave to boil for 40 minutes.
  2. Chop up Onions, Carrots (and Bell Peppers if you want them).
  3. Turn on the oven to preheat at 180°C.
  4. Wet a pan with some cooking oil and brown the mince and onions.
  5. Optional: Add the bell peppers to fry as well.
  6. Add the tomato puree to the pan and mix in.
  7. Mix the Coleman's mix with 300ml of hot water, and add to the pan.
  8. Mix the Oxo cube with the remaining 190ml of hot water, and add to the pan.
  9. Add the carrots and the peas and leave to simmer for 15 minutes.
  10. Once the potatoes are finished, drain the water and mash them with 250g of cheese.
  11. Once finished, pour the mix into a large tray and even out. Distribute the potatoes over the top.
  12. If not grated, grate the remaining cheese and sprinkle on top.
  13. Place the tray in the oven and leave to bake for 30 minutes.
  14. Remove and enjoy!
