All Day Breakfast

Description:

Enjoy making this British classic that makes a great start to the day!

Ingredients

  • Cooking Oil
  • Beans (Branston is best! Screw Heinz!)
  • Sausages of your choice (Richmond's are quite nice)
  • Bacon (Smoked stuff if you like!)
  • Hash Browns
  • Black Pudding
  • Tin of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Bread
  • Optional: Mushrooms (yuck)
  • Optional: Eggs (yuck)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Empty the beans and tomatoes into separate pans and put under low heat.
  3. Put sausages, hash browns and black pudding in to (a) tray(s) and place in oven.
  4. Wet a pan with cooking oil and fry the Bacon. Crispy is best!
  5. Put the bread in the toaster and toast it.
  6. Optionally do the eggs and mushrooms - I'm sure you can work those out.
  7. Assemble everything on to a plate and enjoy!
